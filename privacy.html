Personal data submitted through this website is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiry about the industrial land offered on this site

We will only contact you in relation to the inquiry you initiated. We do not send marketing communications unless you have explicitly opted in.

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data is kept confidential. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except where required by law or where disclosure is necessary to complete a transaction you have requested (e.g., involving a notary or legal counsel during a purchase process).

5. Cookies and Analytics

This website may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site. Google Analytics collects anonymized usage data, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Browser type and device information
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)

Google Analytics uses cookies — small text files stored on your device. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect the core functionality of this website.

For more information on how Google processes data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Storage and Security

Personal data submitted through this website is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. Inquiries and associated data are typically retained for up to 24 months after the last communication, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — You may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing — You may request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — You may object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at michal.ksiadzyna@a4corridor.com. We will respond within 30 days.
