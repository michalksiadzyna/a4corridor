Why This Location

The A4 motorway corridor between Wrocław and Opole is one of western Poland's most active industrial zones. Electrolux, Essity, MCC, and Autoliv operate within 3 km of these plots. €3.8 billion in confirmed investment is concentrated within a 30 km radius — and 12 infrastructure projects worth €355M+ are currently underway.
