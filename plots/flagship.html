Dual-Zone Advantage

P/UO.4 + U1 — Production and Commercial

The flagship plots carry both P/UO.4 (production and services) and U1 (commercial) zoning under the MPZP. This dual-zone designation provides development flexibility that single-zone plots cannot match:

P/UO.4 — Production Zone

Manufacturing, warehousing, logistics, and production services. The primary industrial designation supporting factory construction, storage facilities, and technical infrastructure.

U1 — Commercial Zone

Commercial and service uses including offices, showrooms, and retail supporting the industrial zone. Enables mixed-use development without zoning modifications — a premium feature in the Polish industrial market.

A buyer can develop the site as a pure production facility, a mixed production-and-commercial complex, or segment the dual zones for different functions. This flexibility broadens the investment's potential use cases and future resale market.

Plots 133 (18,369 m²) and 135/1 (9,015 m²) are offered as a bundled acquisition at a unified price. The bundling reflects:

For a buyer planning a facility of 10,000+ m² with supporting infrastructure, the flagship plots provide the necessary scale in a single transaction.
