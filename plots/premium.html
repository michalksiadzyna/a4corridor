Ideal Use Cases

What 9,322 m² Accommodates

The premium plot provides sufficient area for a focused industrial operation. At 9,322 m² (just over 1 hectare), the site can accommodate:

Manufacturing Facility

A production building of 3,000–5,000 m² with surrounding access roads, parking, loading docks, and green space. Suitable for component manufacturing, assembly operations, or specialized production lines.

Warehouse and Distribution

A logistics or distribution facility with truck access, loading bays, and office space. The P/UO.4 zoning supports both warehousing and the service functions typically associated with distribution operations.

The plot sits within the same corridor zone as the flagship and entry plots, enabling potential expansion through future acquisition of nearby parcels if the buyer's needs grow.

P/UO.4 Zoning

Production and Services

P/UO.4 zoning under the permanent MPZP permits manufacturing, production, warehousing, logistics, and ancillary service functions. The zoning is pre-approved — the buyer proceeds directly to the building permit stage without requiring any zoning decision, public hearing, or administrative approval.

This regulatory certainty means the timeline from land acquisition to construction start is determined by architectural design and building permit processing — typically 3–6 months — with no zoning-related delays.
