P/UO.4 Zoning

Production and Services

P/UO.4 zoning under the permanent MPZP permits manufacturing, production, warehousing, logistics, and ancillary service functions. The zoning is pre-approved — the buyer proceeds directly to the building permit stage without requiring any zoning decision, public hearing, or administrative approval.

This regulatory certainty means the timeline from land acquisition to construction start is determined by architectural design and building permit processing — typically 3–6 months — with no zoning-related delays.
