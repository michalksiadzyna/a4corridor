The entry plot is contiguous with the premium plot (143, 9,322 m²) and the flagship plots (133+135/1, 27,384 m²). A buyer acquiring the entry plot today retains the option to expand through subsequent acquisition of adjacent parcels — growing from 4,143 m² to up to 40,849 m² without relocating.
