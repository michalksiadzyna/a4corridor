Local Area

Where Your Employees Will Live

The plots are located in gmina Oława, a well-connected community 25 km from Wrocław. The area offers affordable housing, quality education, healthcare access, and excellent transport links — supporting employee recruitment and retention.

Living Standards

Quality of Life

Oława is a growing community that benefits from proximity to Wrocław while maintaining lower living costs. Employees at corridor facilities can live in Oława, in surrounding villages, or commute from Wrocław — all within practical distances.

The area offers a mix of urban convenience and rural calm. Oława's town center provides daily necessities — supermarkets, restaurants, banks, and services — while the surrounding countryside offers open space and a quieter lifestyle.

Transport

Connections

Rail to Wrocław

Oława Główna station provides 15–25 minute train service to Wrocław Główny (central station). Both Intercity and POLREGIO services operate on this route, with regular daily departures. The new Oława Zachodnia station, opened June 2025, provides additional rail access.

Road Access

The A4 motorway provides direct high-speed access to Wrocław (25 km), Opole, Katowice, and Kraków. The DK94 national road runs parallel as a secondary route. County Road 1572D provides direct access to the plots.

Wrocław Airport

Wrocław Copernicus Airport (WRO) is approximately 35 km from the plots, offering domestic and international flights. Direct connections to major European hubs support business travel for management and visiting clients.

Local Commute

Bus services connect Oława to surrounding communities. The compact size of the area means most employees live within a 15–30 minute commute of the plots, whether driving, cycling, or using public transport.

Education

Schools and Universities

Oława provides primary and secondary education facilities including public and private schools. For higher education, Wrocław's 20+ universities and institutions are accessible within 30 minutes by train or car.

Wrocław University of Technology, Wrocław University, and the University of Economics produce engineering, science, business, and technical graduates who form the recruitment pipeline for manufacturing operations. Access to this talent pool is a significant advantage for employers in the corridor.

Healthcare

Medical Access

The Oława area provides healthcare through local clinics, a hospital, and specialist facilities. For advanced medical care, Wrocław's major hospitals and specialist centers are accessible within 30 minutes. The healthcare infrastructure supports employee welfare and family needs.

Housing

Affordable Living

Housing costs in the Oława area are significantly below Wrocław city center prices. Employees can purchase or rent apartments and houses at rates that support a comfortable standard of living on manufacturing-sector wages. This affordability aids both recruitment and retention.

The area's proximity to Wrocław means that employees who prefer urban living can commute from the city, while those seeking more space and lower costs can live locally. This flexibility broadens the available labor pool beyond any single location.

Recreation

Lifestyle and Environment

The surrounding countryside provides natural recreation — cycling, walking, and outdoor activities. The Odra River and its tributaries run through the area, providing waterfront access. Wrocław offers cultural amenities — theaters, museums, restaurants, and nightlife — all within 25 km.

The combination of accessible urban culture and local natural environment creates a balanced quality of life that supports employee satisfaction and long-term retention.
