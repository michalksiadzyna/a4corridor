Common Features

All Plots Include

Permanent MPZP Zoning

All plots carry legally binding production zoning under the local spatial plan. No WZ application required. No rezoning risk. Proceed directly to building permit.

County Road 1572D Access

Direct access via County Road 1572D. Connected to the DK94 national road and A4 motorway network.

UKUR Exempt

Production zoning exempts all plots from agricultural trade restrictions. Standard transaction process. No KOWR pre-emption. No special permits required.

PIT-Free

All three plots are exempt from personal income tax on sale (acquired 2018, 5-year threshold passed). Clean fiscal status for the seller.
